About XAUUSD

XAUUSD is the spot price of gold in US dollars. It is highly volatile and reacts strongly to:

  • US interest rates and Federal Reserve expectations.
  • Inflation data and real yields.
  • Risk-on / risk-off market sentiment.
  • US dollar strength / weakness.
  • Geopolitical risk and macro news.

This dashboard is for learning and experimentation only. Always backtest and demo-trade before risking real money.
